#E-Commerce Customer Behavior Dashboard

##Overview

Analyzed customer purchasing behavior using 500,000+ online retail transactions to uncover insights on revenue trends, customer segments, and product performance. Built an interactive Power BI dashboard to support data-driven marketing decisions.

---

##Tools Used

* Excel 
* Python
* Power BI 

---

##Key Features

* Revenue, Orders, Customers, Avg Order Value (KPIs)
* Monthly Revenue Trend
* Top Products by Revenue
* Revenue by Country
* Customer Segmentation
* Revenue vs Orders Trend

---

##Key Insights

* UK contributes the majority of revenue
* Sales peak during Nov–Dec (seasonal demand)
* High-value customers drive most revenue
* Few products contribute disproportionately to sales
